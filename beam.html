Beam Design Tool

This tool aids in the design of steel beams utilizing the LRFD method.

How to use

  1. Enter the length of the beam.
  2. Enter the factored linear load applied to the beam.
  3. Select the desired beam size.
  4. Click the 'Calculate' button to show the design results.
